# About

*This is the way. Why is the secret.*

MrlyProd, or Marley Productions, is a small non-profit. It builds open software, hardware, and standards instead of another walled product.

The idea: give physical things a shared mathematical language, the way TCP/IP gave the internet a shared standard nobody owns. The work draws on fractal mathematics, modular design, and powerful magnetics.

- Never acquired, never sold
- Funded by donation
- Everything published freely

Visit [mrly.net](https://mrly.net).
